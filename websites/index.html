Bio Blurb

Red Robin, not related to the restaraunt chain. I'm the third person to have the Robin identity--and the best one in my opinion. My predecessors wish they were as good at their job as me. I am a former member of Young Justice and the Teen Titans. Although other people have had the identity of Red Robin, the current version (me) is the most well-known for the hero name. I am featured in multiple comic series within the DC universe as a side character and have my own serialized comics where I star as the main character which is currently ongoing. My first appearance was in Batman #436 in 1989. I regularly defeat and put away the local bad guys of Gotham, including the Joker, The Riddler, and Two-Face. When I'm not doing detective work and putting criminals behind bars, I'm doing regular people activities and not much else.
